Cipher

It is a mystery quiz, in which you have to solve some puzzles and clues to reach the end. Each Chapter has a hint associated with it. You have a total of 5 lives to complete the quiz. If you use a Hint, one life will be deducted, and if you enter a wrong answer, two lives will be deducted. Try to complete the quiz with as many lives as possible.
